How this works: The estimated Callaghan Creek water level is calculated from a linear regression model trained on field observations from 2023–2025 (n=118, R²=0.815). Formula: Callaghan (m) = 0.0181 + 0.0413 × Fitzsimmons discharge (m³/s). Valid for Fitzsimmons flows of 1.5–21.2 m³/s. Levels: <0.1m too low · 0.1m minimum · 0.2m nice low · 0.3–0.4m prime · 0.4–0.5m advanced · 0.5m+ Kemble level. This is an estimate only — always verify conditions before paddling.
